Sunnyvale Community Solar Farm

Solar Operational ID: SOL-2022-001

Project Overview

The Sunnyvale Community Solar Farm is a 5MW solar installation established in 2022, designed to provide clean, renewable energy to over 1,000 homes in the Sunnyvale community. The project features advanced solar panel technology with optimized tracking systems to maximize energy capture throughout the day.

This community-owned project allows local residents to invest in and benefit from renewable energy production without needing to install panels on their own properties. Excess energy is fed back into the grid, providing credits to community members and supporting the regional transition to clean energy.

Community Impact

The Sunnyvale Community Solar Farm has had a significant positive impact on the local community since its establishment:

  • Reduced electricity bills by an average of 15% for participating households
  • Created 28 permanent jobs for operation and maintenance
  • Generated $350,000 in annual tax revenue for local infrastructure
  • Educational programs have reached over 1,200 students through site tours
  • Carbon offset equivalent to planting 127,000 trees annually
